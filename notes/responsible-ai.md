## What is Responsible AI?
Responsible AI means designing and using AI systems in a way that is ethical, transparent, fair, and accountable.

## Key risks in AI systems
- Bias and discrimination
- Lack of transparency
- Privacy and data protection issues

## Why AI governance matters
AI governance helps organizations identify and manage risks, ensure legal compliance (e.g. GDPR, EU AI Act), and build trust.
